Cora's top 3 favorite dog parks near St.Louis, MO

3. Bark Park (Cottleville, MO)

This dog park is third on the list. It provides lots of space to run and play. It also had very friendly dogs to play with. It is located in the cute town of Cottleville and is a great option for people close to the area!

2.Lake 370 Dog Park (St.Charles, MO)

This dog park is fantastic. It offers agility training equipment, a super large space to run and play, along with a lake that has special access for dogs to swim. It is a super unique and Cora never leaves this dog park with out being exhausted. It is a great place to bring your dog to get out some energy!

1.Bar K (St.Louis, MO)

Bar K definitely takes the cake! It offers both fun for the dog and the dog mom and dads. This place is never not packed with other pups looking to play. It is a hug space with lots of activities to keep the dogs entertained with lots of activities to keep the dogs entertained.
